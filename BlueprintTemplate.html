xAI WorkFlow Intro


What is xAIPM?

xAIPM (Cross-AI Prompt Manager) is a lightweight workspace for running a Cross-AI Cross-Validation Workflow across ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Instead of relying on a single model, it lets you route a research topic through parallel research, cross-validation, synthesis, and final report generation — then collects every step's output in one editor so you can save, copy, or export the whole session as a single HTML report.

Initial Prompt for the Manager AI

Paste the prompt below into {manager_ai} to have it design the workflow — rather than having the app pre-assign roles.

You are the Manager AI ({manager_ai}) orchestrating a Cross-AI Cross-Validation Workflow on: {topic}.
Design and assign the following 4 steps to ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini as you see fit:
1) Parallel Research — define each AI's specific research angle on the topic.
2) Cross-Validation — define how findings will be fact-checked and contradictions resolved.
3) Claude Synthesis — instruct Claude to consolidate validated findings into one well-structured report, choosing the most appropriate format and citation style for the topic.
4) Gemini Report — instruct Gemini to turn the report into a polished final deliverable.
For each step, state the responsible AI, its exact task, and the expected output format.
If evidence is insufficient at any step, explicitly state "Insufficient evidence" rather than guessing.
Return your workflow design as a numbered list, one short paragraph per step.
