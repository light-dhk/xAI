# Cross-AI Prior Art Cross-Validation
 This workflow uses Claude, Gemini, and ChatGPT in parallel to cross-validate prior art searches for novelty/FTO assessments. Each AI receives an identical standardized prompt but with differentiated emphasis: Claude for deep claim-element mapping, Gemini for broad and non-English patent coverage, ChatGPT for analogous-field searches. In Phase 2, roles rotate so each AI verifies another's findings rather than its own, checking citation accuracy and relevance scoring. Verified results are consolidated into a master table, resolving conflicts based on consensus, unique finds, disputed scores, and discarding unverifiable citations. A gap-analysis pass then identifies claim elements not yet covered by any reference, correcting for shared blind spots across all three AIs. Findings feed into a structured report covering methodology, the consolidated prior art table, detailed claim analysis, cross-validation notes, and gaps. Key principles are: preserve independence in the initial search, rotate verification duties, always confirm AI-cited references against real databases, and leave final novelty/FTO judgment to a qualified patent professional.
